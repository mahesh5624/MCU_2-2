Movie Overview

Spider-Man: Homecoming (2017) follows Peter Parker, played by Tom Holland, as he navigates high school life while trying to prove himself as a superhero. After his encounter with the Avengers, Peter is eager to join their ranks and faces off against the villainous Vulture, played by Michael Keaton. The film combines action, humor, and the challenges of growing up.

Key Details

  • Cast: Tom Holland, Michael Keaton, Zendaya
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Comedy
  • Release Date: July 7, 2017
  • Runtime: 133 minutes
