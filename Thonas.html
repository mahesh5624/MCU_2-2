About Thanos

Thanos is one of the most feared and powerful villains in the Marvel Universe. Driven by his belief that the universe's resources are finite, he seeks to restore balance through extreme measures.

Abilities

  • Superhuman strength, durability, and intellect
  • Mastery of advanced technology
  • Wielder of the Infinity Gauntlet with all six Infinity Stones
