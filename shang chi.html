Movie Overview

Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings (2021) follows Shang-Chi, a skilled martial artist who is drawn into the mysterious Ten Rings organization. As he confronts his past, he must face the villainous forces that threaten his world and discover the truth behind his family's legacy. The film stars Simu Liu as Shang-Chi, Awkwafina as Katy, Tony Leung as Wenwu, and Michelle Yeoh as Jiang Nan.

Key Details

  • Director: Destin Daniel Cretton
  • Cast: Simu Liu, Awkwafina, Tony Leung, Michelle Yeoh
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Fantasy
  • Release Date: September 3, 2021
  • Runtime: 132 minutes
