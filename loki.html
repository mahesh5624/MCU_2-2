About Loki

Loki, the God of Mischief and brother to Thor, is known for his cunning, manipulation, and occasional moments of heroism. Whether causing chaos or saving the day, Loki remains an enigmatic figure in the Marvel Universe.

Abilities

  • Mastery of magic and illusions
  • Superhuman strength and agility
  • Shapeshifting and manipulation
