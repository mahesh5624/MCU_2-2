Movie Overview

Doctor Strange (2016) follows the story of Stephen Strange, a talented but arrogant neurosurgeon whose life changes forever after a car accident damages his hands. Desperate to heal himself, he discovers the hidden world of magic and alternate dimensions. Under the guidance of the Ancient One, Strange learns to harness mystical powers and becomes the Sorcerer Supreme, protecting the world from dark forces. The film stars Benedict Cumberbatch, Chiwetel Ejiofor, and Tilda Swinton.

Key Details

  • Director: Scott Derrickson
  • Cast: Benedict Cumberbatch, Chiwetel Ejiofor, Tilda Swinton
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Fantasy
  • Release Date: November 4, 2016
  • Runtime: 115 minutes
