Bruce Banner, a brilliant scientist, was exposed to high levels of gamma radiation, which caused a horrific transformation within him. Whenever he experiences extreme stress or anger, he transforms into the Hulk, a giant green-skinned behemoth with immense strength and destructive power. As the Hulk, Banner loses control over his human self, becoming a force of nature who can devastate everything in his path.
