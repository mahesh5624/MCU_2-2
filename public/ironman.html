About Iron Man

Tony Stark, better known as Iron Man, is one of the most iconic heroes in the Marvel Universe. Armed with his genius intellect, unparalleled knowledge of technology, and a high-tech suit of armor, he fights to protect the world from evil forces.

Abilities

  • Genius-level intellect
  • Expert engineer and inventor
  • Advanced weaponry in his suit
  • Flight and super strength via the Iron Man suit
