Movie Overview

Eternals (2021) follows a group of immortal beings who have secretly lived on Earth for thousands of years, protecting humanity from their ancient enemies, the Deviants. As they come out of hiding, they must work together to confront a new threat that could endanger all of humanity. The film stars Gemma Chan as Sersi, Richard Madden as Ikaris, Angelina Jolie as Thena, and Salma Hayek as Ajak.
