Movie Overview

Iron Man (2008) is the first film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). The movie follows Tony Stark, a genius billionaire inventor, who is kidnapped by terrorists. He creates a suit of armor to escape, and later, he uses his new technology to fight against injustice. The film stars Robert Downey Jr. as Tony Stark/Iron Man, Gwyneth Paltrow as Pepper Potts, Jeff Bridges as Obadiah Stane, and introduces the world to the superhero who would become a symbol of strength and leadership in the MCU.

Key Details

  • Director: Jon Favreau
  • Cast: Robert Downey Jr., Gwyneth Paltrow, Jeff Bridges
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi
  • Release Date: May 2, 2008
  • Runtime: 126 minutes
