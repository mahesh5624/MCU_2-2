Movie Overview

Avengers: Civil War (2016) sees the Avengers split into two factions led by Captain America and Iron Man as they face the consequences of their actions and a government mandate to register as superheroes. This film introduces Spider-Man and Black Panther into the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

Key Details

  • Directors: Anthony Russo, Joe Russo
  • Cast: Chris Evans, Robert Downey Jr., Scarlett Johansson
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi
  • Release Date: April 12, 2016
  • Runtime: 147 minutes
