Movie Overview

Ant-Man and The Wasp (2018) follows Scott Lang as he balances his home life as a father with his responsibilities as Ant-Man. When Hope van Dyne and Dr. Hank Pym present an urgent new mission, Scott must put on the suit and learn to fight alongside The Wasp. The film explores themes of partnership and redemption, bringing a unique blend of humor and action.

Key Details

  • Director: Peyton Reed
  • Cast: Paul Rudd, Evangeline Lilly, Michael Douglas
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Comedy
  • Release Date: July 6, 2018
  • Runtime: 118 minutes
