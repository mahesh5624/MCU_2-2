Movie Overview

Black Widow (2021) follows Natasha Romanoff as she confronts the darker parts of her past and the forces that seek to bring her down. Set after the events of "Captain America: Civil War," the film explores her ties to the Black Widow program and her fight against a conspiracy tied to her history. The film stars Scarlett Johansson as Natasha Romanoff, Florence Pugh as Yelena Belova, David Harbour as Alexei Shostakov, and Rachel Weisz as Melina Vostokoff.

Key Details

  • Cast: Scarlett Johansson, Florence Pugh, David Harbour, Rachel Weisz
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Thriller
  • Release Date: July 9, 2021
