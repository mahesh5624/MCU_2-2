Movie Overview

Thor: Ragnarok (2017) follows Thor, played by Chris Hemsworth, as he finds himself imprisoned on a distant planet. He must team up with his former ally, Hulk (Mark Ruffalo), to stop the powerful Hela (Cate Blanchett) from destroying Asgard. Directed by Taika Waititi, the film blends action, humor, and stunning visuals, showcasing Thor's journey of self-discovery.

Key Details

  • Director: Taika Waititi
  • Cast: Chris Hemsworth, Tom Hiddleston, Cate Blanchett
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Comedy
  • Release Date: November 3, 2017
  • Runtime: 130 minutes
