Movie Overview

Thor: Love and Thunder (2022) sees Thor embarking on a quest for inner peace. However, his retirement is interrupted by Gorr the God Butcher, a new villain who seeks the extinction of the gods. Joined by his old friends and a new ally, Jane Foster, who wields Mjolnir, Thor must face this threat and discover what it means to be a hero. The film stars Chris Hemsworth, Natalie Portman, Tessa Thompson, and Christian Bale.

Key Details
