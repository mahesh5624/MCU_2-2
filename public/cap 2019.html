Movie Overview

Captain Marvel (2019) is a groundbreaking film that follows Carol Danvers, a former U.S. Air Force pilot, as she becomes one of the galaxy's most powerful heroes. Set in the 1990s, the film explores her journey to uncover her past and her connection to the Kree and Skrull conflict. The film stars Brie Larson as Carol Danvers/Captain Marvel, alongside Samuel L. Jackson as Nick Fury.

Key Details

  • Director: Anna Boden, Ryan Fleck
  • Cast: Brie Larson, Samuel L. Jackson, Ben Mendelsohn
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi
  • Release Date: March 8, 2019
  • Runtime: 123 minutes
