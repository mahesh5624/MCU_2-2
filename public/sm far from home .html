Movie Overview

Spider-Man: Far From Home (2019) follows Peter Parker as he embarks on a school trip to Europe. However, he must step up to take on new threats, including the mysterious Mysterio, while grappling with the loss of Tony Stark. The film stars Tom Holland as Spider-Man, Zendaya as MJ, Jake Gyllenhaal as Mysterio, and Samuel L. Jackson as Nick Fury.

Key Details

  • Director: Jon Watts
  • Cast: Tom Holland, Zendaya, Jake Gyllenhaal, Samuel L. Jackson
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Comedy
  • Release Date: July 2, 2019
  • Runtime: 129 minutes
