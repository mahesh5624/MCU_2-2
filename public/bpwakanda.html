Movie Overview

Black Panther: Wakanda Forever (2022) honors the legacy of the late T'Challa while exploring the people of Wakanda as they face new challenges from external threats. As a new hero emerges, the film delves into themes of grief, resilience, and the importance of community. The movie stars Letitia Wright, Lupita Nyong'o, Danai Gurira, and Tenoch Huerta.

Key Details

  • Director: Ryan Coogler
  • Cast: Letitia Wright, Lupita Nyong'o, Danai Gurira, Tenoch Huerta
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Drama
  • Release Date: November 11, 2022
  • Runtime: 161 minutes
