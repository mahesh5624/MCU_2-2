Movie Overview

Iron Man 3 (2013) follows Tony Stark as he grapples with the aftermath of the events in New York from *The Avengers*. With his world shattered, he embarks on a journey of self-discovery and faces the mysterious and powerful Mandarin. The film explores themes of identity, vulnerability, and resilience, showcasing Stark's growth as a hero.

Key Details

  • Director: Shane Black
  • Cast: Robert Downey Jr., Gwyneth Paltrow, Guy Pearce, Ben Kingsley
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi
  • Release Date: May 3, 2013
  • Runtime: 130 minutes
