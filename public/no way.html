Movie Overview

Spider-Man: No Way Home (2021) follows Peter Parker as he grapples with the fallout of his identity being revealed. In his quest to restore his secret life, he seeks help from Doctor Strange, leading to unintended consequences that unleash villains from other universes. This film features Tom Holland as Spider-Man, Zendaya as MJ, and Benedict Cumberbatch as Doctor Strange, bringing together an epic multiverse experience.

Key Details

  • Cast: Tom Holland, Zendaya, Benedict Cumberbatch
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi
  • Release Date: December 17, 2021
  • Runtime: 148 minutes
