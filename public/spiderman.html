About Spider-Man

Peter Parker, known as Spider-Man, is a young hero with extraordinary spider-like abilities. Balancing life as a high school student and a superhero, he protects New York City from various villains.

Abilities

  • Wall-crawling and super agility
  • Spider sense for detecting danger
  • Super strength and speed
  • Web-shooting devices he invented
