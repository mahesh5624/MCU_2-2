Movie Overview

Ant-Man (2015) follows Scott Lang, a former criminal who is recruited by Dr. Hank Pym to help protect his technology that can shrink people down to the size of ants. Equipped with the Ant-Man suit, Scott must pull off a heist that will save the world and become the unlikely hero he was meant to be.

Key Details

  • Cast: Paul Rudd, Michael Douglas, Evangeline Lilly
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Comedy
  • Release Date: July 17, 2015
  • Runtime: 117 minutes
