Movie Overview

The Marvels (2023) brings together Captain Marvel, Ms. Marvel, and Monica Rambeau in an action-packed adventure as they face new cosmic threats. The film explores their unique powers and the bonds that unite them. Starring Brie Larson, Teyonah Parris, and Iman Vellani, it promises to deliver an exciting continuation of the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
