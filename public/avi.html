Movie Overview

Avengers: Infinity War (2018) is the culmination of the Marvel Cinematic Universe's first ten years, bringing together the Avengers and their allies to face the mighty Thanos, who is on a mission to collect all six Infinity Stones. The film explores themes of sacrifice, teamwork, and the fight against overwhelming odds, featuring a star-studded ensemble cast.

Key Details

  • Directors: Anthony and Joe Russo
  • Cast: Robert Downey Jr., Chris Evans, Scarlett Johansson
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi
  • Release Date: April 27, 2018
  • Runtime: 149 minutes
