Iron Man 2 Overview

Iron Man 2 (2010) is the sequel to the 2008 blockbuster *Iron Man* and the third film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). Directed by Jon Favreau, it stars Robert Downey Jr. as Tony Stark/Iron Man, Gwyneth Paltrow as Pepper Potts, Don Cheadle as James "Rhodey" Rhodes, Scarlett Johansson as Black Widow, and Mickey Rourke as Ivan Vanko/Whiplash. The film picks up where the first one left off, with Stark dealing with his newfound fame as a billionaire superhero while facing new threats.

About the Film

The film follows Tony Stark as he struggles with the government's demand to hand over the Iron Man suit, while dealing with his own declining health. Meanwhile, a vengeful Russian inventor, Ivan Vanko (Whiplash), emerges and develops his own version of the Iron Man technology to defeat Stark. The story also explores the relationship between Tony Stark and his best friend, James "Rhodey" Rhodes, as they team up to stop Vanko's evil plot.
