Movie Overview

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 (2017) continues the adventures of Peter Quill and his ragtag team of intergalactic misfits. As they traverse the cosmos, they unravel the mystery of Peter's parentage, all while dealing with new threats and an ever-growing family dynamic. The film stars Chris Pratt, Zoe Saldana, Dave Bautista, and introduces new characters, including Ego, the Living Planet, played by Kurt Russell.

Key Details

  • Director: James Gunn
  • Cast: Chris Pratt, Zoe Saldana, Dave Bautista, Kurt Russell
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Comedy
  • Release Date: May 5, 2017
  • Runtime: 136 minutes
