Movie Overview

Black Panther (2018) follows T'Challa, played by Chadwick Boseman, who returns to the isolated African nation of Wakanda to take the throne as king. When an old enemy reappears, T'Challa must team up with allies to prevent Wakanda from being dragged into a world war. The film celebrates African culture and showcases powerful performances, stunning visuals, and a gripping storyline.

Key Details

  • Director: Ryan Coogler
  • Cast: Chadwick Boseman, Michael B. Jordan, Lupita Nyong'o
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi
  • Release Date: February 16, 2018
  • Runtime: 134 minutes
