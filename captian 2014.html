Movie Overview

Captain America: The Winter Soldier (2014) follows Steve Rogers as he struggles to embrace his role in the modern world while battling a new enemy known as the Winter Soldier. With the help of Black Widow and new allies, he uncovers a conspiracy within S.H.I.E.L.D. that threatens global security.
