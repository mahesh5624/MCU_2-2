Movie Overview

Thor: The Dark World (2013) follows Thor as he battles an ancient race led by Malekith who seeks to plunge the universe back into darkness. With the help of Jane Foster, Thor must unite the realms and fight against an overwhelming force to protect all he holds dear.

  • Cast: Chris Hemsworth, Natalie Portman, Tom Hiddleston, Anthony Hopkins
